Required Monthly Meetings

Required monthly meetings are 30 minute to 1 hour long sessions in which the parent(s) and/or the student meet with me to set goals that will guide us through our future tutoring sessions. This is a time in which we only talk about the student's experience and academic progress (such as grades or scores) with their plan. With this, I am able to collect valueable information about the student and improve their session plan. This is required because it is crucial to address what works and what doesn't early on, and I want to focus on the academic material during tutoring sessions, so students get the most out of it. Furthemore, I can separately meet with the same student to strategize and build better studying habits as often as needed. If the student's plan solely focuses on the college admission process, their sessions merge with these required meetings. Finally, I recommend having check-in meetings more often if the student is struggling with their testing or academic habits.
